About Us

Screaming Coyote is a family run bar and restaurant located in the Mariposa Housing Community in Rio Rancho. Locally owned and operated by lifelong New Mexicans, we are here to serve the community that has served us our entire lives. We offer a New Mexican themed casual style cuisine, coupled with a comprehensive yet creative drink/cocktail menu which will satisfy all. A lovely and sleek indoor dining area, along with the stunning views of the Sandia Mountains on our patio perfectly encapsulate the beauty of the community in which we live. We warmly welcome all and look forward to serving you!
